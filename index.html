About Me

I am a skilled engineer passionate about data analysis, visualization, and data transformation, with a focus on environmental solutions. With over six years of experience in coding for data analysis, I specialize in transforming data into information with temporal and spatial analysis using Python as my primary tool. Adaptable and quick to learn, I thrive on delivering results. As a generalist I enjoy applying my knowledge to diverse projects and fields. My self-motivation, resilience, and commitment to sustainable solutions set me apart. I am eager to contribute to a team that values environmental preservation and positive global impact, leveraging my versatility and adaptability.

My bachelor's degree in Oceanography allowed me to understand the environment as a whole. Studying the dynamics of coastal environments from mangroves to sandy beaches; understanding the geological formation of cliffs and islands; analyzing water quality in rivers and estuaries; connecting the atmosphere and the oceans through wave generation, heat exchange processes, and the ocean acidification process. Those are some of the concepts an oceanographer can bring to the table.

Furthering my knowledge, my master's degree added an engineering perspective to my projects. I collect various types of data directly from the field and institutional instruments to analyze and understand the environment. Based on these findings, I propose sustainable tourism development plan for a highly seasonal coastal city in Brazil and offer solutions based on the Building with Nature concepts to address coastal erosion challenges.

I firmly believe that everything is connected, from the ocean to the land, and we should consider the bigger picture when facing local issues. With the effects of global warming affecting us all, I am eager to use my data and environmental experience to make positive changes in people's life. I am actively seeking opportunities to work on urban management, green projects, and finding ways to addapt to the new challenges ahead.

If you are searching for someone to contribute with data-driven solutions who is passionate about the environment and dedicated to sustainability, I would be excited to join your team. Together, let's create a greener and more resilient future.
